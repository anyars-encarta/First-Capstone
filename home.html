QUALITY IMPROVEMENT
SUMMIT 2023

An interactive, educative, informative and project oriented Quality Improvement Summit aimed at educating top management officials, CEOs, Managing Directors and Human Resource Managers on Quality Improvement skills. The purpose of this project and practical oriented summit is to foster in participants a sense of making quality a top priority in their organizations' corporate policies, visions and missions.

2023.07.25(MON) ~ 28(FRI)

@ International Conference Center, Accra, Ghana, West Africa

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to experienced Quality Improvement advocates talk about and share their experience in their various fields of quality improvements.

QI Exhibition

Explore and appreciate the Quality hallmarks achieved by organizations and institutions that adopted and made quality improvement their topmost prority.

Forum

Interact one-on-one with Quality Improvement enthusiasts with several years of working experience in the field of quality assessment and enforcement.

Workshop

Experience a practical hands on assessment of quality documents, policies and procedures to learn more about how to employ quality in your organisation.

QI Ignite

Interact and network with people from different countries, cultures and background after the summit.
